We want to share our expertise

We know how difficult the whole process of applying to colleges, finding your area of interest, and figuring out a path for success can be. That's why we have a mentorship program.

Benefits of joining

- Be paired up with a current undergraduate student at a CUNY or SUNY school of your choice.
- Take advantage of our valuable firsthand experience to help you find the school/major of your best fit.
- Get help scheduling a visit to a college campus for tours and more!
