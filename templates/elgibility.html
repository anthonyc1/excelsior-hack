Cutting the cost of college

To be elgible for free college tuition, you must be a full-time student enrolled at a SUNY or CUNY two- or four-year college.

This initiative would be phased over a three-year period:
- Starting fall of 2017, any New Yorker making up to $100,000 will qualify.
- Then in 2018, that margin will be increased to $110,000.
- Finally in 2019, the goal to bring tuition-free education in a public university to any New Yorker making up to $125,000.
