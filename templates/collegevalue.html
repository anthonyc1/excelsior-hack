The Value of a College Education

A high school diploma isn't worth what it's used to.

To succeed in today's economy, getting a college education is a MUST. It's estimated that by 2024, about half of all jobs will require a college diploma.

A college degree can be expensive.

In 2015, the average student debt was over $29,000. Take advantage of Cuomo's intiative for free college tution and start your path to getting higher education today.
