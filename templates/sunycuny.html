College

Commuting to college is possible:

  • 93% of all people in New York live within 15 miles from a State Univerisity of New York (SUNY) school.
  • 100% live within 30 miles from a SUNY school.

Opportunities are boundless:

  • There's over 5000 undergraduate academic programs in 64 SUNY campuses all over the NY state to satisfy the breadth and depth of your interests.
  • In addition, there are 24 university campuses as part of the City University of New York (CUNY) system to choose from located throughout the NYC urban area.
