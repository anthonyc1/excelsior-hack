A College Education

Success in today's economy

A high school diploma is not enough in today's economy. It's estimated that by 2024, about half of all jobs will require a college diploma.

Governor Cuomo's plan of action

Governor Cuomo leads an initiative called Excelsior Scholarship that seeks to reduce the burden of college tuition for New Yorkers by making all New York State public universities tuition-free.

Take advantage of Cuomo's intiative for free college tution and start your path to getting higher education today.

Our Mentorship Program

We want to share our expertise

We know how difficult the whole process of applying to colleges, finding your area of interest, and figuring out a path for success can be. That's why we have a mentorship program.

Benefits of joining

  • Be paired up with a current undergraduate student at a CUNY or SUNY school of your choice.
  • Take advantage of our valuable firsthand experience to help you find the school/major of your best fit.
  • Get help scheduling a visit to a college campus for tours and more!

An Open Forum For You

Have questions or concerns?

Take advantage of our open forum where you can interact with other interested students, parents, and current CUNY and SUNY undergrad. The goal is that you can get any question answered and any concern addressed.

Advantages of our forum

  • No need for sign-ups.
  • Connect with other interested students. Develop a network even before you start college!
  • For parents of students, take this opportunity to reach out to other parents.
  • Forum is monitored by students on a day-to-day basis and you will get appropriate feedback in a timely fashion.
